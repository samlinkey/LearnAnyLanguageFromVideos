This software lets you learn any language by watching videos, well I mean masking subtitles when watching bilingual videos, acting as an "electronic sticker".

How to use:

Move Position:     Click and drag any area of the window to move the window
Change the size:  Move the mouse over the window to the bottom right corner, click on the window and drag to move the window
Hide Sticker :       Hold down the Ctrl key to hide, release the display
Exit the software: Ctrl + X
